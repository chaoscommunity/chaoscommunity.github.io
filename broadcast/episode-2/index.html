Episode 2: The one with Russ Miles

Notes:

Russ Miles is a prolific writer and speaker on Chaos Engineering, especially well-known as an evangelist of the discipline in his home town of London. James and Casey discuss his chapter in Chaos Engineering: System Resiliency in Practice which is titled, “Open Minds, Open Science, and Open Chaos.” And as if that isn’t a mouthful, we discuss his previous published works and his Chaos Engineering-themed tour of the United States.

Russ Miles

Russ is CEO of ChaosIQ. Russ founded the Chaos Toolkit open source project along with Sylvain Hellegouarch. He is an international speaker and author, most recently having published Chaos Engineering Observability and Learning Chaos Engineering from O’Reilly Media.

Keywords and phrases

Gamedays, Chaos Toolkit, Scientific Method, Hunting Surprises, Dungeons and Dragons, Oxford, Sadistic Person in the Room, Guitar, North Umbria, Rippon, ChaosIQ, Oxford, Human Error, Continuous Verification, and Oxford.
